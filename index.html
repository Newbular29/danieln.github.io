About Griffin

Data Scientist and Leader

I am a Data Scientist who is passionate about advanced and functional analytics and leadership in an evolving technical world. My passions for Data Science and leadership intersect to create my drive for actionable insights and mentorship of those around me. I began my career as a Mathematical Economics and Applied Mathematics double major at Hamdpen-Sydney College, and then entered the Masters of Science in Business Analytics (MSBA) Data Science program at The College of William and Mary. After graduating I moved into the pharmaceutical industry specializing in operations with a distinct focus on providing functional insights to and partnering heavily with salesforces. My ideal is to provide insights that guide business decisions at every turn - boosting the access to curated data for key decision-makers while pioneering a sustainable infrastructure and flexible data environment.
